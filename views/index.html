Groceryify

Welcome to the Groceryify!
After logging in, you can make a grocery list with a group of friends or family.
Once someone buys the grocery item, it can be double-clicked to be moved to the fridge or pantry.
The app can also suggest when to repurchase items based on how long they have been in your fridge or pantry.

Enjoy the convenience of always knowing what food you have at home in an effort to avoid food waste!
