NOTE: If you try to log in to an account which has not been created yet, your data will not be saved.
NOTE: Refresh the page first if you have to find another group

Add an item to your grocery list below:


  1. Add details to your form about the grocery items you would like to buy.
  2. Click on the "Submit" button after filling out the form.
  3. You will notice that your grocery item gets added to your cart.
  4. If you wish to reduce the quantity of a specific grocery item, simply click on the minus (-) button next to it.
  5. If you wish to delete a specific grocery item, click on the delete or trash button next to it.
  6. Once you have purchased the item, double click on that item in your cart and it will automatically get transferred to either the Fridge or the Pantry.
  7. We will notify you when 1, 2, or, 3+ weeks have passed by changing the color of your grocery item to
    • Yellow: 1 week
    • Orange: 2 weeks
    • Red: 3+ weeks
  8. Since this is a group grocery list, you can chat with your groupmates by clicking on the "Chat" button located on the top right corner of the page.
